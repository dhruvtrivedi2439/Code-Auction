ABOUT HOUSE CUP

While you are in the department, your achievements will earn you house points, while any rule-breaking will lose house points. At the end of the year, the house with the most points is awarded the house cup, a great honor. You will be given points on actions that reflect well on a student's house, such as academic achievements and extracurricular activities.

Each House will have an equal number of students from each year.

Each member of the House can contribute independently to their own House.

The points will be awarded to that House with its achiever's name.

Rule Book

Each House contains an equal number of students from each year. Each member of the House can contribute independently to their own House. The points will be awarded to that House with its achieverâ€™s name. Certificates obtained and events participated in by the students in the Academic Year 2023-2024, i.e. from July 2023 to May 2024, will only be counted for awarding points. At the end of each Academic Year, a Prize Distribution Ceremony will be held. This ceremony will be entertaining, and the efforts made by the students will be awarded. The House with the maximum number of points wins the House Cup. The top scorers in their respective houses will also receive exciting prizes.

Rules with the points being awarded as follows:

  • 20 points: Participation in any event outside of the college. (Sports and Extracurricular Activities).
  • 25 points: Participation in any event within the college. (Sports and Extracurricular Activities).
  • 45 points: Attending workshops, trainings or seminars outside the college concerning academics.
  • 50 points: Attending workshops, trainings or seminars in the college.
  • 75 points: PBL, NPTEL, and spoken tutorials.
  • 80 points: Appearing for certification examination outside of the college (Coursera, Udemy,etc).
  • 90 points: Internship (Selection Letter Required).
  • 100 points: Completion of certification examination under APSIT skills.
